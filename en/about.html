Our story

BoardGame Online Shop started in 2018, from a small group of friends who reserved every Friday for a board game night. What began as a shelf with a few boxes grew over time into a shop with more than 300 titles and regular tournament evenings. Today we serve players across Serbia and organise promotions and workshops for all ages.

Our mission

We believe the best moments happen around a table, away from screens. Our mission is to help every group find the right game — whether it's relaxed family fun, a tense strategic battle or a quick round full of laughter. We try, through advice and not just sales, to make the world of board games accessible to everyone.

Awards and recognition

We are proud of the recognition we have earned over the years.

  • Best specialised game shop — community choice 2021.
  • Organiser of the year at the regional board game festival 2022.
  • Recognition for promoting board games among young people 2023.
